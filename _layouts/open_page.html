Akhil Sathuluri

I currently work as an Electrical Engineer in the Robotics and Automation team of Bajaj Auto Ltd., the largest three-wheeler and the third largest two-wheeler manufacturer in the world. I work on electrical sub-system design for special purpose robotic machines and edge based machine vision systems.

I graduated from the Department of Engineering Design, Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I was a member of the Robotics Lab, advised by Prof. Sandipan Bandyopadhyay, where I worked on the topics of design, analysis and control of parallel robots.
