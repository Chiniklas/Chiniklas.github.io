Chi Zhang

I am a master student at Technical University of Munich, working on reinforcement learning based control of modern robotics.

In the year 2022, I worked as a research assistant at the Robot Systems Group at Laboratory for Product Development and Lightweight Design (LPL) at School of Engineering and Design of TU Munich.

I finished my master thesis in the year 2023 at Robotics Innovation Center(RIC) at German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence(DFKI) under the supervision of Felix Wiebe, Akhil Sathuluri, Prof. Dr. Shivesh Kumar ,Prof. Dr. Frank Kirchner, and Prof. Dr. Markus Zimmermann.

I graduated from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, China University of Mining and Technology.
